Social Media
and College Stduents

The Internet has made it possible for people to connect using different social platforms available online. Social networking sites are becoming popular each day. About 75% of the American adult population use one social media or the other. Young people are the greatest users of social media. The continued use of social media has introduced new patterns of socialization. Unlike in the past, people can create relationships in the virtual space. Although social media became popular in the twenty-first century, there is evidence that social networking sites have existed since the 1970s. However, the initial social networking sites did not gain much publicity. The situation in the contemporary day is extremely different. Social networking sites are popular and have changed the way people communicate. Social media sites serve as evidence of how globalization has created an interconnected village compared to the globe that existed before.

How do College Students use Social Media?

An online survey was conducted at the University of Miami to see how college

students there use social media. The survey was distributed via email, a total

of 38 responses were collected.

100% of them use social media.

The survey consists of 11 questions, complete survey can be found at

https://www.surveymonkey.com/r/LGK5HZC.

All respondants are current enrolled students at the University of Miami from

age 18 to 29. 50% are female and 50% are male.

In the last decade, social media usage has increased remarkably. Before 2006, only about 5-10% of adults were using social media platforms. By 2006, the number had risen to about 25%. In 2008, more than 35% of adults in America were using social media sites. Between 2008 and 2010, the number increased remarkably to above 50%. Mainly, about 60% of American adults were using social media in 2010. Between 2012 and 2013, the number of users had increased to about 75% of adults. After 2014, the number has continued to grow beyond 75% of American adults. In other countries, the use of social media has also risen in the last decade. Young people aged 18-29 years are the greatest users of social media. Facebook has the largest number of users followed by Twitter. These two social networking sites have proven to be the most interactive.
